Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data AIAA collects, why it collects it, how it is used, who it is shared with, and how you can request deletion. It is written to be clear, complete, and consistent with applicable data protection laws including GDPR and Indian data protection requirements.

This page is publicly accessible and not geo-blocked. The URL of this page is registered in the Meta App Dashboard privacy policy field as required by Meta Platform Terms Section 4.

1. Who we are

Business name: Sai Ved / AIAA - AI Agents and Automation Agency

Contact: ved.work@outlook.com

AIAA provides AI-based customer support automation, document intelligence, and WhatsApp Business messaging integration services. When you use this service, AIAA acts as the data controller for your account and business data.

2. What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate and improve the service.

Account and identity data

  • Your name, email address, and password used to create and access your AIAA account.
  • Company or business name provided during signup.

WhatsApp and Meta platform data

  • WhatsApp Business Account ID, phone number ID, and business ID collected during Meta Embedded Signup onboarding.
  • OAuth access tokens issued by Meta to enable WhatsApp Cloud API message delivery.
  • Incoming and outgoing WhatsApp message content processed through your connected business account for the purposes of AI-assisted automation.
  • Phone numbers of end customers who message your WhatsApp Business number.

Uploaded documents and files

  • PDF documents and files you upload to AIAA for AI indexing and retrieval.
  • Text chunks and vector embeddings derived from those documents, stored in a vector database for search and retrieval.

Chat and session data

  • Messages and prompts sent through the AIAA website chat interface.
  • In-memory session history maintained during active chat sessions.

Technical and operational data

  • API request logs, timestamps, and error logs used for debugging and service reliability.
  • Document processing status and metadata such as file names, upload times, and indexing status.

3. How we use your data

We use collected data only for the following legitimate purposes and never beyond them:

  • Providing the service: authenticating your account, processing uploaded documents, indexing content into the vector database, and enabling AI-powered question answering.
  • WhatsApp automation: sending and receiving messages through your connected WhatsApp Business number, identifying the correct tenant, and routing AI responses to the right customers.
  • Service operation and security: debugging errors, preventing abuse, monitoring performance, and protecting against unauthorized access.
  • Compliance: meeting Meta Platform Terms, applicable Indian and international data protection law, and platform policy requirements.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties. We do not process platform data for purposes other than those described above.

4. Meta platform data - specific disclosures

AIAA accesses Meta platform data through the Meta for Developers Platform. The following applies specifically to that data as required by applicable platform terms:

  • Platform data from Meta is processed only for the purposes described in this policy and only as authorized by you during onboarding.
  • We do not sell, license, or purchase platform data.
  • We do not use platform data to make eligibility determinations, to build user profiles for unrelated purposes, or to perform surveillance.
  • We do not attempt to re-identify, de-anonymize, or reverse-engineer any platform data.
  • Meta onboarding is completed through Meta-controlled Embedded Signup interfaces. We do not collect Facebook or Meta login credentials directly.
  • Access tokens issued by Meta are stored in our database only to enable ongoing WhatsApp API operation and are not shared with unauthorized parties.
  • You may revoke our access to your Meta platform data at any time through your Meta Business Suite settings.

5. Data sharing and third-party service providers

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data is shared only with the following service providers strictly necessary to operate the service:

Authentication and database
Supabase

Stores account data, tenant records, document metadata, and WhatsApp configuration. Provides authentication services.

Vector search
Pinecone

Stores vector embeddings derived from your uploaded documents for AI-powered semantic search and retrieval.

AI model inference
OpenRouter / LLM providers

Receives chat messages and document context to generate AI responses. Only the relevant query and context are sent, not full account data.

Messaging platform
Meta Platforms, Inc.

WhatsApp Cloud API is used to send and receive business messages. Data sharing with Meta is required by the WhatsApp Business Platform and is governed by Meta's own terms.

Hosting and infrastructure
Railway / cloud hosting

Hosts the application backend. Has access to infrastructure but not to application-level user data beyond what is required for operation.

All service providers are used only for the purposes described and are not permitted to use your data for their own unrelated purposes.

6. Data retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

  • Account and tenant data is retained while your account is active.
  • Uploaded documents and vector embeddings are retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • WhatsApp session history is held in memory only for the duration of active sessions and is not permanently persisted.
  • API and operational logs are retained for a limited period for debugging and security purposes.
  • Upon a valid deletion request or account closure, all personal data is deleted or anonymized as described in Section 7 below.

7. Your rights and data deletion

You have the right to request access, correction, portability, or deletion of your personal data at any time.

To delete your account and all associated data: visit our data deletion page, log in with your credentials, and confirm deletion. Your account, uploaded documents, indexed files, and WhatsApp configuration will be permanently removed from our systems.

This deletion mechanism is available directly in your browser without needing to contact us by email. This mechanism is directly accessible in your browser without needing to contact us.

For any other data rights requests, contact ved.work@outlook.com.

8. Data security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Authentication and access control using Supabase Auth with token-based sessions.
  • Role-based separation between public API endpoints and admin service operations.
  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Webhook signature verification for Meta callback requests using HMAC-SHA256.

No online system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security issue, contact us immediately at ved.work@outlook.com.

9. Children's privacy

AIAA is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18 years of age. If you believe a minor has provided data to us, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. International data transfers

AIAA uses cloud service providers that may process data outside of India, including in the United States. By using the service, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws. We take reasonable steps to ensure such transfers are protected through appropriate contractual or technical safeguards.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the service, applicable law, or Meta platform requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
